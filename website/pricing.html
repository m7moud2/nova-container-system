Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card for the free tier?

No. Sign up with just your email and start deploying immediately. Credit card required only when upgrading to Pro.

What happens if I exceed my egress limit?

We'll notify you at 80% and 100% usage. Going over adds $0.09 per extra GB for Hobby; Pro users get a grace buffer before overage kicks in.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your billing settings. Proration is calculated automatically to the hour.

What languages are supported?

All plans support Node.js, Python, Go, Rust, and static sites. Nova auto-detects your stack — no Dockerfile, no config yaml needed.

Is there a student or OSS discount?

Yes! Students with a .edu email get Pro free for 12 months. Open-source maintainers can apply for free Pro through our GitHub Sponsors page.

How does the 14-day trial work?

You get full Pro access immediately with no charge. If you don't cancel in 14 days you'll be billed at your chosen billing cycle.
